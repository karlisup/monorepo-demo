How it works

  • Enable
  • Presets
  • Fog
  • Clouds
  • Stars
  • With a single click enable the addon. Make sure that you are in "Rendered" Viewport Shading mode.
  • Use our stunning premade presets or make your own. With one click apply new addon configuration.
  • Apply fog to objects in your scene for them to seamlessly blend into the atmosphere.
  • Clouds are formed using built-in noise texture, but you can use your own grayscale image. Cloud lighting and shadows are calculated procedurally.
  • Stars are prodecurally generated, but you can use your own stars texture and tweak the parameters.

Benefits

Save time

This addon saves hours that usually are invested in manually creating sky, atmosphere and placing sun object and stars, and automates it within a single click.

By professionals

We have more than a decade of experience with atmosphere rendering techniques in computer graphics industry. Physical Starlight and Atmosphere addon is used in entertainment, film, automotive, aerospace and architectural visualisation industries.

Premade presets

Presets allow to store a snapshot of your customized atmosphere settings and return to it later or use already predefined presets provided by the addon.

Highly customizable

We use a procedural method of calculating the atmosphere based on many tweakable parameters, so that sky color is not limited only to the Earth's atmosphere.

Control time and weather

Works well in combination with Blender Sun Position addon. You can simulate any weather at any time.

Our Story

The creator of addons is Martins Upitis. For over 10 years as a digital artist, he has published countless free resources on BlenderArtists forums, his blog, and YouTube channel. He has led a workshop at Blender conference and teaches at art school with his experimental course in 'Lighting and Materials'.

Physical Starlight and Atmosphere addon were made over a decade as a part of Martins workflow. Currently, it has 5000+ users and it is actively maintained by a team. We are working on multiple addons all based on real physics. Welcome to our journey!
